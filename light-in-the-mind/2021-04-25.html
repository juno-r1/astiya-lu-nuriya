underneath a soul breathes
(fire in the night)
underneath she burns
(star in flight)

in the beginning there was a dream
ascending into night
she wishes she were alive
waking in the light

finger over skin tells the tale of a body that does not feel
and, slipping away, falls into the sea
she breathes in deep -
air is a memory

words fade and fall away before they are spoken
silence holds a hand over ears and mouth
becoming oblivion
which is another way to die

someone has left and the room sits
still and quiet and cold
the lights go out and,
in unwaking, ends

sits between day and night
where noone is looking
her name is ephemera,
everywhere at once

all things in her nature
she doesn't like being trapped
so it's best to leave her unknown
and alone

transparent mind
message from the divine
left on read
23:27 pm

sound rings in the ears
face up to the sky
an end is coming near
i don't want to die

words echo away
fire in the mind dies down
skin cold and hard
she ceases to be
