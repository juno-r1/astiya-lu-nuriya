06/08/2021

world into mind
i sense a real that i cannot see
the infinite ends here
all is fantasy

the eye blinds
the ear deafens
the hand numbs
the mind suppresses

do not turn your eye on me
that you might render me less than i am
turn your back on me
and know that i will still be here when you return

songbird in the light
know that your tune is fleeting
and persists only because your flight
turns back the dark, wings beating

have you come to see the lady in the tower?
she isn't taking visitors at this time
it has been so long since we welcomed her
that no-one remains who remembers her name

do you think all this will stay the same? i tell you this:
look upon the ruins and the ashes of the places that have been
and the shoots and saplings growing in the places that will be
and know this: nothing is inevitable.
