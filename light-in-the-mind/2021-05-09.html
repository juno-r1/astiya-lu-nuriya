09/05/2021

i am already beginning to forget
she is someone i could never be
running, escaping, fleeing
and free

all the lights die down
black sky fades to grey
when the night is over
nothing else can stay

morning comes
beset by the tyranny of light
i am not ready for the world
leave me in the night

her face is mine
bright-eyed and alive
but when the dream departs
the mask, cold and dead, returns

we were not made for infinity
we do not bear its weight
memory takes the bits it wants
and throws the rest away

my name is my own
but i am many things
and a simple word does not suffice
to describe me

memories over memories
past over past
the mind is a knife and it cuts through me
like i'm barely there at all

many minds in one
bleeding over my eyes
too loud to bear
i am losing the real

i remember most things
good or bad
but i am beginning to forget
how they fit together

i am screaming inside my mind
every day for nine years
and i have never broken
i wish i could remember how to stop
